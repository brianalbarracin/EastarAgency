Commercial Auto Coverage Options

Liability Coverage

Protects your business if your vehicle causes injury or property damage to others while being used for business purposes.

Collision Coverage

Comprehensive Coverage

Protects against non-collision incidents like theft, vandalism, fire, hail, or animal collisions.

Hired Auto Coverage

Protection for vehicles your business rents, leases, or borrows for commercial use.
